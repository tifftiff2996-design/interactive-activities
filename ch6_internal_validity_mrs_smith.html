Chapter 6

Internal validity — spot the threat (MRS SMITH)

Read each flawed research scenario and identify which MRS SMITH threat to internal validity is present.

M — Maturation
Biological/psychological changes in participants over time
R — Regression to mean
Extreme scorers naturally move toward average on retesting
S — Selection
Pre-existing differences between groups before the study
S — Selection × Maturation
Groups mature or change at different rates
M — Mortality
Differential dropout between conditions
I — Instrumentation
Changes in measurement tools or procedures across time
T — Testing
Pre-test exposure affects post-test performance
H — History
External events between measurements affect outcomes
