Chapter 3

Scales of measurement classifier

Identify whether each measure is nominal, ordinal, interval, or ratio. Use the reference cards below.

Nominal
Categories only. No order. No math. (e.g. eye color, gender)
Ordinal
Ranked order. Gaps unequal. (e.g. 1st/2nd/3rd place)
Interval
Equal gaps. No true zero. (e.g. temperature °C, IQ)
Ratio
Equal gaps + true zero. Ratios meaningful. (e.g. height, reaction time)
