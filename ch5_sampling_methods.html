Chapter 5

Sampling methods — identify the technique

Read each study description and identify the sampling method used. Consider the threat to external validity in each case.

Simple random
Every member of the population has an equal chance of selection
Stratified random
Population divided into subgroups; random sample drawn from each
Systematic
Every nth person selected from a list
Convenience
Participants selected because they are readily available
Voluntary / self-selection
Participants choose to take part (e.g. respond to an ad)
Cluster
Groups (clusters) randomly selected; all members of chosen clusters included
